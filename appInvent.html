A Simple Android App

I got the idea for the Android game to the right from an episode of the Office. In the episode, they sit and hope the DVD logo perfectly hits the corner of the screen. The game is played by using the cursor (or if it was on an Android phone, your thumb) to flick the DVD logo to one of the corners of the screen. If you get it into the corner perfectly pat yourself on the back because there is no win condition prompt, we are using the honor system.

There is a really long loading screen of a blank white screen, you can skip past that part of the video.
